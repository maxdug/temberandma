Airports

Key West International (EYW)

Key West International Airport (EYW) is the closest airport. It is a 15 minute drive from Old Town Manor. There are taxis, Uber, and Lyft available to/from the airport.

Miami International (MIA) and Fort Lauderdale International (FLL)

Miami International Airport (MIA) and Fort Lauderdale International Airport (FLL) are both about 4 hours away. The drive down US-1 is very scenic with many bridges connecting the Keys. Consider this option if you plan to stay in the Keys for a longer trip.

Getting Around

On this 4.2 square mile island, everything is within walking distance or just a short bike ride away! No need to rent a car.

Parking

If you do decide to drive, discounted passes to the city garage are offered for $15/day to guests staying at Old Town Manor. The garage is about a 5-10 minute walk from the property. There is also metered street parking just outside the hotel and other paid options in the neighborhood.
