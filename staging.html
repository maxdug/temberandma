Airports

Key West International Airport (EYW) is the closest airport. It is a 15 minute drive from Old Town Manor. There are taxis, Uber, and Lyft available to/from the airport.

Miami International Airport (MIA) and Fort Lauderdale International Airport (FLL) are both about 4 hours away. The drive down US-1 is very scenic with many bridges connecting the Keys. Consider this option if you plan to stay in the Keys for a longer trip.

Getting Around

No need to rent a car. On this 4.2 square mile island, everything is within walking distance or just a short bike ride away!

Parking

If you do decide to drive, discounted passes to the city garage are offered for $15/day to Old Town Manor guests. The garage is about a 5-10 minute walk from the property. There is also metered street parking and other paid options in the neighborhood.

Explore Other Keys

Just under an hour drive from Key West is Bahia Honda. The park has snorkeling and a nature trail with views from the old railroad bridge. Also about an hour away is the Key Deer Refuge where you can spot Key deer, coastal birds, and alligators!

Old Town Manor

Old Town Manor is a historic bed and breakfast in the heart of Key West. Our wedding will be taking place in the private garden that the rooms overlook. Note: at this time, our guests have booked all of the rooms, so check out the options below.

Airbnbs/VRBO

Many affordable vacation properties are available to rent in the Keys via airbnb.com and vrbo.com. This option is ideal if you prefer to stay in a condo or house over a hotel.

Other Hotels

There are several large brand hotels as well as boutique inns in Key West. Consider this option if you want to stay nearby with the amenities of a hotel.
